Welcome to What's For Dinner!

I developed this on my own (just Sage!) With input from friends and family as I created it.

This app solves the age old question - "What do I want for dinner??". It mixes functionality of a personal recipe book with tinder. If you're craving "savory," the app will sort and display your app's recipes in a tinder-style "yes" or "no" format with an image (if you added one) of any recipes tagged "savory". If you have "noodles, beef, chicken, butter", the app will sort recipes in your tinder selection based on how many of those ingredients the recipe has. If you have truly NO IDEA what you want, there is also a random button, which will sort through all of your recipes randomly.

In the recipe book (pre-loaded with some recipies), you can add, edit, and view any recipes you have. Pre-loaded recipies are stored in a .json loaded on install, and anything added is stored with Room.

Currently the app is completely local with no dependencies. In the future, IF I continue to develop this, I'd love to add online functionality. One of my friends suggested a recipe rating system, which I think sounds very useful!

Special shoutout to my club, Struggling Engineers, whose students were willing to submit some recipes!
